About Mining
What is Mining?

Mining is the process of validating and adding pending transactions to the blockchain. It involves solving a computationally difficult problem (Proof of Work).

How it works:
  • All pending transactions are collected into a new block
  • The miner must find a valid "nonce" that makes the block hash start with a specific number of zeros
  • This requires many computational attempts (that's why it's called "work")
  • Once found, the block is added to the blockchain
  • The miner receives a reward for their computational work
